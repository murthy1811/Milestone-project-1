COVID-19 Vaccination - When is your turn?

People who have the greatest need for protection against Covid-19 will be offered vaccination first.

  • Phase 1 : Front-line workers, people in Care homes.
  • Phase 2 : Risk groups with underlying diseases and people aged above 65
  • Phase 3 : All people aged above 18.

How Vaccines work?

  • Most Vaccines contain a small amount of virus that causes the disease
  • The virus is weakened that it can't create any disease
  • After taking Vaccine, our body takes control of virus and create Anti-bodies
  • After a short period of time, You will be immune to the virus

Is Covid-19 Vaccine safe?

  • COVID-19 vaccine has undergone the same kinds of thorough checks as other vaccines
  • Many people have received the vaccine and researchers have studied its effects
  • There were no major side effects found in these studies across the world
  • Authorities have assessed that the benefit of the vaccine outweighs the risks
COVID-19 vaccine is new and developed for a pandemic, so there might be still unknown risks.
